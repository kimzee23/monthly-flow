Blood Group Information

Blood groups are important for safe transfusions, pregnancy, and medical treatments. Learn about compatibility, donation, and why your blood type matters.

A+

Can donate to: A+, AB+

Can receive from: A+, A-, O+, O-

B+

Can donate to: B+, AB+

Can receive from: B+, B-, O+, O-

O+

Can donate to: All positive groups

Can receive from: O+, O-

AB+

Universal recipient (can receive from all)

Can donate to: AB+ only

O-

Universal donor (can donate to all)

Can receive from: O- only
