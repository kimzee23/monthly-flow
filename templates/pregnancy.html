Pregnancy Support

Guidance, care, and tips to support women during pregnancy.

Tips for a Healthy Pregnancy

Nutrition

Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, proteins, and whole grains to support you and your baby’s growth.

Exercise

Engage in light physical activities such as walking or prenatal yoga to maintain energy and reduce stress.

Rest

Get enough sleep and rest to help your body cope with pregnancy changes.

Medical Checkups

Attend regular prenatal checkups for your health and your baby’s development.

Support & Emergency Hotlines

  • Emergency: Call 112 (or your local emergency number)
  • Nearest Clinic: Visit your nearest maternity clinic
